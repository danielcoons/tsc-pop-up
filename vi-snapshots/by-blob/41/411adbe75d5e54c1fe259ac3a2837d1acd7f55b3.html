Pop-Up.lvlib:Popup.lvclass:Convert Enum to Image.vi

This VI will take the input ENUM typedef for the Graphic chosen and will get its image data using the LabVIEW PNG files.

Inputs:

-Graphics [typedef combobox] - the image to look up

-background [2d picture] - the background color for the image

Outputs:

-Image [2D Image] - the image data
